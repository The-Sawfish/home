Overview

What is The Sawfish?

The Sawfish is a comprehensive digital platform that provides browser-based gaming experiences and productivity applications. Our infrastructure is built for performance, reliability, and accessibility. Everything runs directly in your browser—no downloads, no installations, no hassles.

Whether you're looking for entertainment through our extensive game collection or seeking productivity tools for daily tasks, The Sawfish delivers a seamless experience across all devices. Our platforms are optimized for modern browsers and touch-enabled devices, including iPad.

  • Browser-based gaming and applications
  • iPad and touch device optimization
  • No installation required
  • Cross-platform compatibility

Process

How It Works

Getting started with The Sawfish platforms is simple. Follow these steps to access our gaming and application ecosystem.

1

Choose Your Platform

Navigate to either the Game Portal for entertainment or the App Store for productivity applications. Both platforms are accessible directly through your web browser without any installation.

2

Browse & Select

Explore our curated collections. The Game Portal features over 300 web-native games including Minecraft browser edition. The App Store offers productivity tools and utilities.

3

Launch Immediately

Click to launch any game or application instantly. Everything runs in-browser with no additional software required. For mobile devices, optionally add to home screen for app-like experience.

4

Enjoy & Create

Play games, use productivity tools, and explore all features. Our platforms support save states, progress tracking, and cross-session continuity for supported applications.

Applications

PWA Applications

Our applications are Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) that work best when installed to your device.

Important: Add to Home Screen Required

For the best experience and full functionality, these applications must be installed to your device's home screen. Simply visiting the website is not enough—installation removes the browser address bar and provides a native app-like experience.

1 Visit App Store
2 Tap Share Button
3 Select "Add to Home Screen"
